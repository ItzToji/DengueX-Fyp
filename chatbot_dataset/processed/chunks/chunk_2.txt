Prevent mosquito bites Dengue is spread to people through the bite of an infected mosquito . Mosquitoes bite during the day and night . The best way to prevent dengue is to protect yourself from mosquito bites . Use insect repellent . Wear loose-fitting , long-sleeved shirts and pants .
